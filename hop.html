MONEY NEED

The importance of money is clear when you factor in the costs of necessities to sustain life. It can't buy happiness, but financial wellbeing is important if you want to provide for your family's basic needs. Many or all of the companies featured provide compensation to LendEDU. These commissions are how we maintain our free service for consumers. Compensation, along with hours of in-depth editorial research, determines where & how companies appear on our site. At some point in your life, you’ve probably heard someone say they don’t care about money; you may have even said that yourself. While this sentiment sounds nice in theory, the reality is — for better or worse that you shouldn’t underestimate the importance of money.
